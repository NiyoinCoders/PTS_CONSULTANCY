GCC Consulting

Global Capability Center (GCCs) are also referred to as Captives, Global In-House Centers (GICs), Global Shared Services Centers (SSC), Global Technology Centers (GTC), Global Competence Centers. GCCs are fully owned by parent organization and integrated India hubs typically established in talent-rich locations to build significant value and intellectual property (IP) using collaborative and distributed teams.

Our offering of end-to-end products include sourcing, hiring and engaging the best talent; customized workspaces with integrated IT; providing compliance, governance and operational support. We use our proven implementation consulting Services to establish and support the GCC in any of the following methodologies:

In-House GCC

Outsourced GCC
